Resume

I am a student at the University of Cincinnati studying graphic communication design with a focus in Interaction Design and an interest in Service Design.

I've worked at the Mayo Clinic Center for Innovation helping design the future experience of healthcare and Kareo, a medical software startup helping small practice doctors run their practice.

I participated in a collaborative studio designing in-flight communication systems for Boeing with the LiveWell Collaborative, Designed Crowdhealth for the Putting Care in Context Design Challenge & took part in a two day Collaborative Design Workshop lead by Frog

I love finding ways to simplify complex systems, and am always curious to explore what I can learn from other industries, disciplines and people.
